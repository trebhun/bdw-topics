html semantics

What are html semantics?

Html semantics are the use of html to enforce the webpage's information and meaning, and they are elements that describe the meaning to both the browser and the developer.

Why are html semantics important?

Html semantics are meant to separate presentation and content, as a way to maintain orgranization and clarity for webpages. They are elements suchas form, table, or img that clearly define its content. In contrast, non-semantic elements would tell us nothing about their content, like div or span. Good semantics mean that a html document outline is well organzied and can be easily viewed by a blind reader. This means that the semantics help to make the information accesible and when it is marked up properly the structure of a document is clear.

New Semantics in html5

  • section
  • article
  • footer
  • progress
